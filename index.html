Football FAQs

Who has won the most FIFA World Cups?

Brazil holds the record with 5 FIFA World Cup titles, winning in 1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, and 2002.

Who is considered the greatest footballer of all time?

Opinions vary, but Lionel Messi and Pelé are frequently mentioned as the greatest, with Messi recently winning a World Cup to further strengthen his case.

What is the UEFA Champions League?

The UEFA Champions League is Europe’s premier club football competition, where top teams from each league compete for the most prestigious title in European club football.
